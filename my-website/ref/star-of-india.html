Star of India

Located on: 1659 W. North Temple, Salt Lake City

Star of India Restaurant in Salt Lake City proudly serves Northern Indian cuisine, featuring authentic Tandoori cooking, the "cuisine of the emperors," and one of the most refined styles of regional Indian fare. This delectably seasoned Indian cuisine style is a favorite of the most discriminating lovers of Indian food. All of our dishes are prepared fresh on the premises from natural ingredients. Among other Indian delicacies, we specialize in chicken curry, lamb, goat, seafood, halal meat, and pure vegetable entrees. All items are deliciously spiced to your taste -- from very mild to very hot. Be sure to save room for dessert as Star of India has one of the largest selections of Indian sweets in the area
