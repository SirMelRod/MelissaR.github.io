Baek Ri Hyang

Located on: 3390 South State Street Ste 31 South Salt Lake, UT 84115

Baek Ri Hyang is a Korean Chinese restaurant located in South Salt Lake Utah. The restaurant seeks to offer authentic Korean and Korean Chinese dishes such as bulgogi, galbi, eun dae gu jorim, naeng myeon, ja jang and rich more. The business originally hails from Las Vegas.
